Our Story

SpiceTrail Restaurant was founded in 2010 by renowned chef Michael Laurent with a simple vision: to create an exceptional dining experience that combines culinary artistry with warm hospitality.

What began as a small bistro has evolved into one of the city's most respected dining establishments, known for its innovative dishes and commitment to quality.
